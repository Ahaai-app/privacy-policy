Privacy Policy

AhaAI
Effective Date: May 25, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 25, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how AhaAI ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. AhaAI allows you to photograph homework problems and receive AI-generated solutions.

We are committed to your privacy. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data, your images, or any information about your device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any of the following:

  • Photos or images you submit through the App
  • Device identifiers or device information
  • Location data
  • Usage or behavioural data
  • Crash logs or diagnostic data

3. How Image Processing Works

When you photograph a homework problem, the image is sent directly to OpenAI's API to generate a solution. The image is processed by OpenAI and the result is returned to you. We do not see, store, or have access to any images you submit.

OpenAI's handling of data is governed by their own Privacy Policy, which you can review at openai.com/privacy.

4. Information We Do Collect

The only information we collect is what is necessary to manage your subscription:

  • Your email address
  • Your subscription status (active, expired, etc.)

This information is used solely to verify your access to the App and to communicate with you about your subscription (e.g., renewal notices, billing issues). We do not share this information with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their email address to create a subscription, please contact us at admin@ahaai.com and we will remove it promptly.

6. Data Security

We store subscriber email addresses and subscription status securely. We do not store payment details — all payments are handled by our payment processor, whose own privacy policy applies to that data.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Request to see the data we hold about you (your email and subscription status)
  • Request deletion of your data

To make a request, email us at admin@ahaai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. App Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Camera — To photograph homework problems. Only activated when you initiate a scan. Images are not stored by us.
  • Internet Access — To send your image to OpenAI's API and return the solution to you.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and notify subscribers by email where appropriate. Your continued use of the App after changes are made constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
